What is Python?

Or better yet, Why Python?

Background

  • Properly indented pseudocode
  • Around since 90s
  • Science-compatible "ideology"
  • Readable
  • Rust/C/C++/FORTRAN compatible

Where is Python?

  • Google, Apple
  • US National Labs
  • International research projects

How to Python?

  • Installs on any almost device (many already)
  • Read python.org
  • Check out scipy.org
  • Use stackoverflow.com
  • Get trigger happy with Google

So... Why Python?

  • Clean, quick, readable
    • bugs/line roughly constant over languages
  • Extensive (and extensible) capabilities
    • general and employable
  • Huge amount of information
    • plenty of help solving problems
  • Lots of scientific users
  • Easier to pick up than most common alternatives...
